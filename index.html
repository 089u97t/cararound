    CarsAround is a cutting-edge logistic application designed to provide a HASSLE-FREE and costeffective transportation service for users while offering drivers the opportunity to register with any vehicle without incurring any percentage charges. The app will prioritize user convenience, safety, affordability, and personalized driver selection options to enhance the overall experience. With CarsAround, users can benefit from a user-friendly platform that allows them to easily access comfortable transport, estimate costs based on distance and location, determine amounts set by drivers based on local transportation, make immediate payments, and stay updated on real-time order status. CarsAround offers efficient tools for managing incoming orders, providing a selection of available drivers, and gaining valuable insights through a comprehensive dashboard. This document provides an extensive overview of the key features and functionalities of the CarsAround app. Users Roles: 1. Customer: Individuals who register on the platform to order for rides, select their preferred drivers. 2. CarsAround Administrator: Responsible for configuring system settings, managing user accounts, and overseeing software settings.
